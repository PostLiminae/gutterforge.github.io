What is The Gutter?

The Gutter is a science fiction setting etched into the husk of a shattered multiverse—where cosmic horror is not a genre, but a constant state of being. It is the outermost trench of reality, warped by entropy and curiosity. This project explores hyperrealistic systems such as expansion bay modules, ontological anomalies, blacksite engineering, and post-identity threats.

From malfunctioning ontological nurseries to cults of quantum recursion, this is a setting that asks: "What breaks first—the ship, or the soul?"
